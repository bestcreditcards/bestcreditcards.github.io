The Best Qantas Credit Cards for Grocery Spending

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Grocery shopping is a necessity, but with rising food prices and inflation hitting households hard, every dollar counts. If you’re a frequent shopper at supermarkets like Woolworths, Coles, or Aldi, using the right Qantas credit card can turn your everyday spending into valuable rewards. Whether you’re stocking up on essentials or indulging in gourmet treats, these cards can help you earn Qantas Points faster while keeping your budget in check.

Why Use a Qantas Credit Card for Groceries?

Maximizing Rewards on Essential Spending

Groceries are one of the biggest recurring expenses for most households. By using a Qantas-linked credit card, you can earn points on every dollar spent, effectively turning your weekly shop into future flights, upgrades, or even gift cards. With food prices soaring globally due to supply chain disruptions and climate-related challenges, getting extra value from your spending is more important than ever.

Inflation-Proofing Your Budget

With inflation driving up the cost of living, every smart financial move helps. A Qantas credit card that offers bonus points or cashback on supermarket purchases can offset some of the financial strain. Some cards even provide additional perks like purchase protection or extended warranties, adding extra security to your spending.

Top Qantas Credit Cards for Grocery Spending

1. Qantas Premier Platinum Credit Card

Key Features:

  • Earn 2 Qantas Points per $1 spent at major supermarkets (Woolworths, Coles, Aldi, and more).
  • Complimentary Qantas flight voucher each year (valued at up to $450).
  • Travel benefits including lounge passes and travel insurance.

Why It’s Great for Groceries:

This card is ideal for families or frequent shoppers who want to maximize rewards on essential spending. The annual flight voucher alone can cover a domestic return trip, making the card’s fee worthwhile.

2. Qantas American Express Ultimate Card

Key Features:

  • Earn 3 Qantas Points per $1 at supermarkets (uncapped rewards).
  • Annual travel credit of $450 to use on Qantas flights.
  • Complimentary airport lounge access and comprehensive travel insurance.

Why It’s Great for Groceries:

With one of the highest earn rates for supermarket spending, this card is perfect for big households or those who prefer premium rewards. The travel credit effectively offsets the annual fee, making it a no-brainer for frequent flyers.

3. Qantas Money Signature Card

Key Features:

  • 1.25 Qantas Points per $1 on all purchases, including groceries.
  • No international transaction fees, great for specialty grocery imports.
  • Flexible repayment options with low-interest promotions.

Why It’s Great for Groceries:

If you shop at international grocery stores or online markets, the lack of foreign transaction fees makes this card a winner. The flat rewards rate ensures steady point accumulation.

How to Choose the Right Card for Your Grocery Needs

Consider Your Spending Habits

  • Big Spenders: If your monthly grocery bill exceeds $1,000, prioritize cards with higher earn rates (like the Qantas Amex Ultimate).
  • Occasional Shoppers: A no-annual-fee card with a lower earn rate may suffice.

Watch for Bonus Offers

Many Qantas cards offer sign-up bonuses (e.g., 50,000 bonus points after meeting a spend requirement). Time your application to maximize these deals.

Check for Supermarket Partnerships

Some cards offer extra points when shopping at specific retailers. For example, Woolworths and Qantas have a long-standing rewards partnership—using a linked card can stack rewards.

Additional Ways to Boost Qantas Points on Groceries

Use Qantas Shopping Portal

Before heading to the store, check if your supermarket is listed on the Qantas Shopping portal. Purchasing gift cards or shopping online through the portal can earn bonus points.

Link Your Loyalty Programs

If you’re a Woolworths Rewards member, link your account to Qantas Frequent Flyer to convert Woolworths points into Qantas Points.

Pay Attention to Promotions

Qantas often runs seasonal promotions where grocery spending earns extra points. Keep an eye on your email or the Qantas app for limited-time offers.

The Future of Rewards Spending

As digital payments and contactless transactions become the norm, credit card rewards are evolving. Some experts predict that dynamic currency rewards (where points adjust based on spending categories) could become standard. For now, choosing a card that aligns with your grocery habits remains the best strategy.

With the right Qantas credit card, your grocery bills can do more than fill your pantry—they can help you take your next vacation. Whether you’re flying domestically or dreaming of an international escape, every swipe at the checkout brings you closer to your next adventure.
