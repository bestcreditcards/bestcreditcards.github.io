How 717 Credit Union’s Business Accounts Boost Local Economy

Home / Blog / Blog Details

In today’s rapidly changing economic landscape, small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) are the backbone of local communities. Yet, many struggle to access the financial tools they need to grow and thrive. That’s where 717 Credit Union steps in. By offering tailored business accounts and financial services, 717 Credit Union doesn’t just support individual businesses—it fuels the entire local economy.

The Power of Community-Focused Banking

Why Local Financial Institutions Matter

Big banks often prioritize large corporations, leaving SMBs with limited options. In contrast, credit unions like 717 Credit Union operate with a member-first philosophy. They reinvest profits back into the community, creating a ripple effect that strengthens local economies.

How Business Accounts Make a Difference

717 Credit Union’s business accounts come with features designed for SMBs:
- Low fees and competitive interest rates – Reducing overhead costs for businesses.
- Personalized financial advice – Helping entrepreneurs make informed decisions.
- Flexible lending options – Supporting expansion and innovation.

By providing these resources, 717 Credit Union ensures that local businesses have the capital and guidance they need to succeed.

Strengthening Local Supply Chains

Supporting Small Suppliers

When businesses bank with 717 Credit Union, they’re more likely to reinvest in other local vendors. This creates a self-sustaining economic ecosystem where money circulates within the community rather than flowing out to multinational corporations.

Encouraging Sustainable Practices

Many SMBs today prioritize sustainability, but green initiatives often require upfront investment. 717 Credit Union’s business loans and grants help local companies adopt eco-friendly practices, from solar panels to zero-waste packaging.

Job Creation and Workforce Development

Funding New Hires

Access to capital allows businesses to expand their teams. A single small business loan from 717 Credit Union can lead to multiple new jobs, reducing unemployment in the area.

Upskilling Employees

Some of 717 Credit Union’s business accounts include workforce development grants, helping employers train staff in high-demand skills like digital marketing or renewable energy tech.

Adapting to Economic Challenges

Navigating Inflation and Supply Chain Disruptions

With rising costs and global instability, many SMBs face cash flow issues. 717 Credit Union’s emergency business lines of credit provide a safety net, ensuring companies can weather economic storms.

Embracing Digital Transformation

The shift to online commerce is accelerating. 717 Credit Union offers fintech solutions, like mobile banking and digital payment integrations, helping local businesses compete in a digital-first world.

Success Stories: Real Businesses, Real Impact

From a Food Truck to a Brick-and-Mortar Restaurant

One local entrepreneur used a 717 Credit Union business loan to transition from a food truck to a full-service restaurant, creating 12 new jobs in the process.

A Green Energy Startup’s Growth

A small solar panel installer secured funding through 717 Credit Union, allowing them to scale operations and serve more households with renewable energy solutions.

The Future of Local Economies

As automation and globalization reshape industries, the role of community-focused financial institutions becomes even more critical. By banking with 717 Credit Union, businesses don’t just secure their own future—they help build a resilient, thriving local economy for generations to come.

The next time you see a "Now Hiring" sign at a neighborhood shop or a new sustainable business opening its doors, remember: behind many of these success stories is a credit union that believed in the power of local growth.
